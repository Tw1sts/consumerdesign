Project Overview

In response to evolving consumer needs and strict regulatory requirements in the vaping industry, IVG Premium E-Liquids sought to develop a groundbreaking product that would stand out in a competitive market while maintaining compliance with international regulations.

The IVG 4in1 device represents a significant innovation in vaping technology. The concept integrates four distinct product configurations into a single modular system, offering unprecedented versatility to users. This revolutionary approach not only enhances the consumer experience but also streamlines manufacturing and distribution processes.

As Lead Designer, I led the entire product development process from initial concept sketches to final production-ready 3D models. The project required navigating complex technical constraints, regulatory requirements, and brand considerations to create a cohesive, premium product.

The result is an award-winning design that has been successfully launched in multiple global markets and has contributed significantly to IVG's market position as an innovation leader in the industry.

The Modular Design Approach

The core innovation of the IVG 4in1 product is its modular architecture. By designing a system of interchangeable components, we created a product that could adapt to different user preferences and regulatory environments without requiring multiple distinct devices.

This approach presented significant technical challenges, particularly in creating connection mechanisms that were both secure and user-friendly while maintaining a premium feel and aesthetic. Through iterative prototyping and rigorous testing, we developed a proprietary locking system that became a signature element of the design.

The modular approach also enabled a more sustainable product lifecycle, as users could replace individual components rather than the entire device, reducing electronic waste and extending the product's useful life.
