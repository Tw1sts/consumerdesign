About Michael Twist

Award-winning Product Concept Designer with a passion for creating exceptional consumer experiences

10+ Years Exp.

Michael Twist

I'm a Senior Product Concept Designer with extensive experience in brand strategy, 3D modeling, and leading design teams. Currently serving as Head of Design at IVG Premium E-Liquids, I oversee all aspects of design work while guiding multi-disciplinary teams to develop innovative products.

My approach combines strategic thinking with creative problem-solving to develop consumer products that not only look exceptional but perform brilliantly for end users. I believe great design should simplify complexity and create meaningful connections between people and products.

With a background spanning across various design disciplines and industries, I bring a holistic perspective to every project. My experience in regulated markets has honed my ability to balance creative expression with compliance requirements - creating designs that stand out while meeting strict industry standards.

Through ConsumerDesign, I aim to share my expertise and collaborate with forward-thinking brands that want to elevate their consumer products to new heights.
